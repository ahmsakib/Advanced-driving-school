About Our Driving School

Professional instruction from experienced teachers in modern, well-maintained vehicles

At Advanced Driving School, we've been shaping confident drivers in Dallas for over 15 years. Our approach combines patience, expertise, and modern teaching techniques to ensure you not only pass your test but become a safe driver for life.

We specialize in helping nervous drivers overcome their anxiety behind the wheel. Our instructors are trained to create a supportive, stress-free learning environment tailored to your individual needs.

All lessons are conducted in late-model vehicles equipped with dual controls for maximum safety, featuring the latest safety technologies like automatic emergency braking and blind spot monitoring.
