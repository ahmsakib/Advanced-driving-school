About Our Driving School

Advanced Driving School, owned and operated by Kazi Islam, has been providing top-quality driving instruction for over 10 years. Our mission is to create safe, confident drivers through patient, professional teaching methods.

We understand that learning to drive can be stressful, which is why our instructors are trained to create a calm, supportive learning environment. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refresh your skills, we'll tailor our lessons to your individual needs.

All our vehicles are modern, well-maintained, and equipped with dual controls for maximum safety during lessons.
